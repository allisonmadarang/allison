Academics of San Francisco High School




San Francisco High School is known
for its rigorous academic programs, diverse
student body, and innovative teaching methods.
The school offers a wide range
of Advanced Placement (AP) courses, honors classes,
and extracurricular activities to cater
to the diverse interests and abilities
of its students. Additionally, the school
has strong partnerships with local universities
and organizations, providing students with opportunities
for internships, research projects, and college
preparation programs. San Francisco High School
consistently ranks among the top high schools
in the state for academic achievement
and college readiness.
